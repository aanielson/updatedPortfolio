Teacher-turned-developer, Abigail is onto her next adventure! She graduated from BYU in 2019 with an English Teaching degree and a minor in Digital Humanities.

If you don't know what the "Digital Humanities" are, you are not alone. Seriously. Basically, Abigail obtained skills in graphic design, print publishing, and website development from her minor.

After teaching English, Aerobic Conditioning, and "Creative Coding" at Diamond Fork Junior High, Abigail thought that coding was SOOOO cool that she just HAD to sacrifice the altruistic teaching lifestyle.

She also ran the "Girls Who Code" club at the junior high. Rather than convince young, impressionable girls to pursue careers in coding, Abigail convinced herself.
